About iPhone

The iPhone is a smartphone made by Apple that combines a computer, iPod, digital camera and cellular phone into one device with a touchscreen interface. The iPhone runs the iOS operating system, and in 2021 when the iPhone 13 was introduced, it offered up to 1 TB of storage and a 12-megapixel camera.For instance, you can use your iPhone to browse the internet, check emails, make calls, track flights, pay bills, watch videos and movies, listen to music, create presentations, write documents and share pictures.
